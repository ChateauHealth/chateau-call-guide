Your Guide to Our Current Call System

Introduction

This document serves as a comprehensive guide to our current phone system and how incoming calls are routed. We utilize a layered system involving Crexendo, CallRail, and for a transitional period, GoTo. Understanding these flows is crucial for effective communication and client support.

üìû 1. Main Line & Admissions Call Flow

Our primary public-facing number, prominently displayed on our website and Google Business account, is 801-877-1272. When a caller dials this number, they first interact with our CallRail automated attendant.

First Attendant (CallRail)

"Thank you for calling Chateau Health & Wellness. If this is a medical emergency or you are in a crisis, please hang up and dial 911 immediately. For information on our services or to begin the admissions process, please press 1. For all other inquiries, including how to contact a loved one in treatment, please press 2."
  • If the caller presses 1 (Admissions):
    • This triggers a CallRail workflow designed to connect to our admissions team.
    • The system first attempts a Simulcall, ringing Colin (801-819-8772) and Tyler (801-669-1248) for 15 seconds.
    • If no one answers, a second Simulcall is initiated, ringing Colin, Tyler, Brad (405-245-5139), and Kevin (385-539-8326) simultaneously.
    • If still unanswered, the call is directed to a CallRail voicemail.
  • If the caller presses 2 (Additional Options):
    • This option forwards the call to our main Crexendo auto attendant (385-534-0836).

Admissions Line Porting Update:

Our dedicated admissions number, 801-507-5031, has successfully ported over from CallRail to Crexendo. However, it is currently configured within Crexendo to forward calls back to the CallRail number 385-203-8631. This setup is specifically maintained for analytical data and business metrics that integrate with our CRM and CallRail.
